Local Observer Registration

To apply as a local election monitor, please ensure you have all the necessary documents ready for upload. Your application will require a certificate of registration from the Registrar of Societies or PACRA in pdf format. Additionally, you'll need to upload: a list of individual monitors in csv format, an introductory letter in pdf format with monitor details, copies of national identification cards or passports in pdf format, and a passport-size photo in jpg or png format. You will also need to provide your institution's full details and contact information.
