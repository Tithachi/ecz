Local Observer Registration

To apply as a local election monitor, please ensure you have all the necessary documents ready for upload. Your application will require:

  1. 1. A certificate of registration from the Registrar of Societies or PACRA in pdf format.
  2. 2. A list of individual monitors in csv format.
  3. 3. An introductory letter in pdf format with monitor details.
  4. 4. Copies of national identification cards or passports in pdf format.
  5. 5. A passport-size photo in jpg or png format.
  6. 6. Institution's full details and contact information.
