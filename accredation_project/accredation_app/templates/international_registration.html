International Observer Registration

To apply as an international election observer, please ensure you have all the necessary documents ready for upload. Your application will require:

  1. 1. A clearance from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation in pdf format.
  2. 2. An estimated list of observers in csv format.
  3. 3. An introductory letter in pdf format with details of the observers.
  4. 4. Copies of national identification cards or passports in pdf format.
  5. 5. Passport-size photos in jpg or png format.
  6. 6. Institution's full details, including the city of stay and lodging arrangements in Zambia.
